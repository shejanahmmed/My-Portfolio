About Me

I'm Farjan Ahmmed (Shejan), pursuing a Bachelor's in Software Engineering at Daffodil International University. I'm driven by a passion for creating intelligent AI tools that simplify life, exploring the frontiers of Machine Learning and AI innovation.

I work with Python, C, Java, HTML, CSS and JavaScript to build intelligent solutions. My notable projects include an automated face recognition attendance system, a DIU PDF cover webapp and a Jarvis AI assistant. I also love hardware projects - I've built smart LED lights with ESP32 and even created a small drone with a webcam.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me reading detective stories (Sherlock Holmes, Feluda, Byomkesh Bakshi), collecting watches and coins, exploring nature or tinkering with cool gadgets. I'm always learning something new and working towards my goal of creating AI models that truly help humanity.
